Call For Papers

Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Vaagdevi College of Engineering feels pleasure to organize an 1st Springer International Conference “Smart Technologies in Communication, Automation and Computing (ICSTCAC-20)”. This conference provides a platform for researchers working on Internet of Things (IoT), Automation and computing. The authors will be having an opportunity to meet their peers working in the same domain.
